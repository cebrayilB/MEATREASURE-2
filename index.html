ABOUT COMPANY

MeaTreasure provides the target market with beef jerky and chicken jerky, and dried beef/chicken meat with spices. It is the mission of MeaTreasure to provide the best product quality, customer service quality, and suitable price. As a result, everyone will be sure that they received the best value and will be loyal customers. As jerky production is not very popular in Azerbaijan, there are only a few companies that produce beef and chicken jerky. The slogan of the company is Meet and Eat the Local Meat! which motivates the local customers to buy a natural local product.

ABOUT PRODUCTS

The product is offered by MeTreasure is beef and chicken jerky, which is made by drying beef and chicken meat with the addition of spices (most commonly salt and pepper Jerky may serve both as a snack to consume with alcoholic drinks and as a snack for athletes. Athletes and kids may consume it as a snack since meat is the main ingredient used for producing beef and chicken jerky and it contains vitamins and proteins which contribute to the health of consumers. Furthermore, jerky meat contains lower levels of fat than ordinary meat does. 4 types of packaging: Single size and Family size packages of beef and chicken jerky are distributed for the usage of consumers. The single size and Family size packages contain 100 gr and 500 gr of jerky, respectively.
